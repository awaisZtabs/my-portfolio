PlayBookAI’s intuitive AI functionality allows for easy, natural language searches, while  users also have the ability to collaborate and annotate on documents in real time across their teams.

Each jurisdiction’s regulations and compliance documents are digitally collated in one easy-to-use platform. The most current version of each document is always available, eliminating the risk of relying on outdated regulations/policies.   

PlayBookAI utilizes the latest developments in artificial intelligence to allow users to ask any compliance question and return answers and AI-generated summaries across multiple jurisdictions instantaneously.

PlayBookAI's Key Features

AI Search Engine

Experience the power of AI with PlayBookAI’s advanced technology, including Natural Language Processing & transformer models. Get instant answers to compliance questions across multiple jurisdictions.

Curated Document Library

Gain access to an extensive and diverse repository of gaming compliance documents. Stay constantly updated with the most current versions, ensuring you never rely on outdated regulations and policies.

Jurisdictional Homepages

Explore dedicated homepages with Regulator Information, Pending Regulations, operators, and jurisdiction-specific Insights. Access the Document Library, including forms and applications, on the jurisdiction's homepage.

Expert Insights

Unlock a wealth of expertly curated answers. Our cutting-edge AI technology provides reliable information extracted directly from source materials. Dive deeper into the context with one-click access to source documents.

Doc Compare

Compare the latest version of a document to previous ones for thorough analysis. Stay informed and up to date with our comprehensive side-by-side comparison feature for tracking regulatory changes.

Alerts

Stay ahead of evolving regulations in the gaming industry. Our document monitoring system scans for updates, and you receive immediate alerts via the Notifications feature when new or updated documents are detected.
