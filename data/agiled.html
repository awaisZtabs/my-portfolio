Frequently Asked Questions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is completely secure. We use multiple firewalls, encryption protocols, and other security safeguards to keep your data safe. You can read more about this here. 

Is Agiled GDPR compliant

Agiled is 100% GDPR compliant, and you can view or export your data at any given time.

Do you have a free plan?

Yes, absolutely. You can start using Agiled for Free. Just signup for a free account and start growing your business. 

How can I capture leads from website using Agiled?

You can easily create a form using a form template in Agiled and embed it on your website or landing page to capture leads. 

How long does it take to get started?

We have built Agiled to be intuitive. We guide you through the steps to get started in Agiled when you sign up for a new account. You can set up your account in less than 20 minutes. 

How can I bring my old data in Agiled?

We have a lot of native integrations with popular platforms that can help you bring your data in Agiled. We also offer CSV data import for almost all sections of Agiled. 

Do you help with Migration?

Yes, absolutely. Reach out to our team at support@agiled.app and we will help you with migration. 

What is a client portal?

Client portal is where you can invite your clients so they can see every thing related to them like projects, invoices, estimates, contracts etc in one place.
